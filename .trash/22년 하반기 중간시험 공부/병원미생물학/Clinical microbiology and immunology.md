4. Prevention and treatment of infectious diseases
	1. Vaccine
	2. [[Herd Immunity]]
		1. Virus spread stops when the probability of infection drops below a critical threshold
		2. The threshold is virus and population specific
			1. smallpox : 80~85% (천연두)
			2. Measles : 93~95% (홍역)
		3. No vaccine is 100% effective
		4. When 80% of population is immunized with measles, 76% of population is immune
	3. Requirements of an effective vaccine
		1. Safety : no disease, minimal side effects
		2. Induce protective immunity in the population
		3. Protection must be long-lasting (지속시간이 길어야함)
		4. Low cost (<1$); genetic stability; storage considerations; delivery (oral vs needle)
	4. RNA vaccine development
	5. Conjugate vaccine
		1. antibody에 antigene을 붙여서 백신으로 이용
